fn set_init_step(&mut self, step: &impl ToInputArray) -> Result<()>

Sets the initial step that will be used in downhill simplex algorithm.

Step, together with initial point (given in DownhillSolver::minimize) are two n-dimensional vectors that are used to determine the shape of initial simplex. Roughly said, initial point determines the position of a simplex (it will become simplex’s centroid), while step determines the spread (size in each dimension) of a simplex.
